ACM Club

The Association for Computation Machinery is a multi-faceted technical chapter involved in guiding amateur engineers towards achieving their goals. It works on numerous technical fronts and has a history of making incredible projects like a Terra-Mapping Sandbox, a motion controller project, among others. BMU ACM student chapter is not merely a club but a family. It has been working towards fostering technology and innovation among enthusiasts. It has conducted numerous events, workshops, and tech talks that garnered applause and awe. The Flagship Event of ACM, HackBMU 5.0, a hackathon focused on bringing up the community of computer enthusiasts to promote innovation, received enormous attention globally. From the celebration of open source Hacktoberfest workshop to hands-on Game Development, from Graphic Designing to intense Coding, from all the fun events to major Tech talks, everything you find is here at BMU ACM!
