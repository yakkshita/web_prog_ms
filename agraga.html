Agraga Club

Agraga - The HR IR Club of BML Munjal University is a student-run club that provides its members with a platform to network, interact, and learn more about the industry specific requirements and aims at building future leaders. The HR Club organizes various activities to develop the participants knowledge, skills, and confidence. These activities include guest lectures, workshops, panel discussions, case studies, and research projects. The club also organizes competitions such as HR Quiz, HR Strategy, and HR Business Plan. It also provides its members with the opportunity to interact with experts from the industry and get an insight into the trends and practices of Human Resources Management. It also organizes industrial visits to give the members an opportunity to gain some hands-on learning experience in the field. Apart from the academic activities, the club also organizes various recreational activities like sports and cultural activities. This helps the members in developing their skills of teamwork and collaboration. The HR Club at BML Munjal University is thus a great platform for the students to gain knowledge about the significance of Human Resource in every field.
