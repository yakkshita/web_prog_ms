Culinary Club

Culinary club provides opportunities to students who love food and are interested in developing their culinary skills. This club helps students socialize and have fun while learning and doing different types of activities based on food. It brings people of the same interest together. It also helps the student bring out their creativity and passion in Gourmet styles. The students are encouraged to participate and help lead the club in cooking activities. Culinary Club provides a platform for students from different ethnicities to interact and improve their knowledge about different styles of cooking. The goal here is to inspire others to pursue their culinary dreams. The club organizes various competitions and workshops which involves food with a flagship event by the name of Masterchef.
