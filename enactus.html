Enactus Club

Enactus is an international organization that connects students, academic and business leaders through entrepreneurial-based projects that empower people to transform opportunities into real, sustainable progress for themselves and their communities. Here at BMU, it strives to do the same. This is a great learning platform for students where practicality meets theoretical knowledge. Students learn a lot in the classrooms, and this is where they can come and put out their learnings into creating something efficient and effective for the greater good of the society. Enactus BMU takes entrepreneurial action for others while using business principles and innovation to further the Global Goals and create a sustainable positive impact on people, planet, and prosperity. Empresario and Speakerporium are the flagship events conducted by the club in the past years along with many other minor events, all of them focused on societal well-being and social entrepreneurship. It will be working on more than 10 SDG’s (Sustainable Development Goals) projects in the upcoming months.
