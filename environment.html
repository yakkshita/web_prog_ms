Environment Club

ENVIRONMENT is the key to life on this earth. We have a stable environment as compared to other planets which makes our earth habitable. Ever thought what will happen or where you will go if one day mother earth gives up on our behavior with her? NO, RIGHT? This is what Environment Club at BMU is here for. It is there to help you take those small steps to prevent you from doing anything wrong to our environment. A healthy atmosphere is crucial for maintaining a calm and stable way of life. Nevertheless, because some individuals are reckless, our environment is becoming more and more contaminated every day. Everyone should give this problem considerable care, especially the younger generation. The Environment Club is a student-teacher volunteer organization that encourages student involvement in environmental education and environmental sustainability initiatives. Its primary objective is to work in areas such as social development and the environment. The initiative by the club emphasizes on increasing teacher and student understanding of the environment at BMU in numerous ways.
